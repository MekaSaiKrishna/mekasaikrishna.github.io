Presented research investigating manufacturing defects in Automated Fiber Placement (AFP) technology. Addressed the combined effects of layup imperfections (gaps and overlaps) and consolidation/curing defects on composite mechanical performance, using the smeared crack approach for progressive damage analysis on AFP-fabricated coupons.
